3.6 Inhomogeneous Systems of Difference Equations

3.6 Inhomogeneous Systems of Difference Equations#

We shall now turn our attention to systems of first order difference equations of the form

(16)#\[ \mathbf{x}_{k+1} = A\mathbf{x}_k+\mathbf{f}_k \]

for which \(\displaystyle\mathbf{x}_0\) is given. The solution of such a system may be found inductively as follows

\[\begin{split} \text{For } t=0 \quad \mathbf{x}_1 &= A\mathbf{x}_0+\mathbf{f}_0,\\ \text{For } t=1 \quad \mathbf{x}_2 &=A\mathbf{x}_1+\mathbf{f}_1 = A(A\mathbf{x}_0+\mathbf{f}_0)+\mathbf{f}_1,\\ &=A^2\mathbf{x}_0+A\mathbf{f}_0+\mathbf{f}_1,\\ \text{For } t=2 \quad \mathbf{x}_3 &= A\mathbf{x}_2+\mathbf{f}_2 = A(A^2\mathbf{x}_0+A\mathbf{f}_0+\mathbf{f}_1)+\mathbf{f}_2,\\ &=A^3\mathbf{x}_0+A^2\mathbf{f}_0+A\mathbf{f}_1+\mathbf{f}_2,\\ &~~\vdots\\ \mathbf{x}_k &= A^k\mathbf{x}_0 +A^{k-1}\mathbf{f}_0+A^{k-2}\mathbf{f}_{1}+\cdots+\mathbf{f}_{k-1},\\ &=A^k\mathbf{x}_0+\sum_{r=0}^{k-1}{A^r\mathbf{f}_{k-1-r}},\\ &=A^k\mathbf{x}_0+\sum_{r=0}^{k-1}{A^{k-1-r}\mathbf{f}_{r}}. \end{split}\]

Alternatively, it could be found by using z-transforms. Taking z-transforms of (16)

\[ z\bar{\mathbf{x}}-z\mathbf{x}_0 = A\bar{\mathbf{x}}+\bar{\mathbf{f}}, \]

which upon rearrangement gives

\[ \left(zI_n-A\right)\bar{\mathbf{x}} = z\mathbf{x}_0+\bar{\mathbf{f}}, \]

or

\[ \bar{\mathbf{x}} = \left(zI_n-A\right)^{-1}\left(z\mathbf{x}_0+\bar{\mathbf{f}}\right). \]

Now,

\[\begin{split} \left(zI_n-A\right)^{-1} &= \left[z(I_n-\frac{1}{z}A)\right]^{-1}=\frac{1}{z}(I_n-\frac{1}{z}A)^{-1},\\ &=\frac{1}{z}\sum_{r=0}^\infty{\left(\frac{1}{z}A\right)^r}. \end{split}\]

Therefore

\[ \left(zI_n-A\right)^{-1}z\mathbf{x}_0 = \sum_{r=0}^\infty{\frac{1}{z^r}A^r\mathbf{x}_0}, \]

whose inverse transform is

\[ \sum_{r=0}^\infty{\delta_{kr}A^r\mathbf{x}_0} = A^k\mathbf{x}_0. \]

Also,

\[ \left(zI_n-A\right)^{-1}\bar{\mathbf{f}} = \sum_{r=0}^\infty{\frac{1}{z^{r+1}}A^r\bar{\mathbf{f}}} = \sum_{r=0}^\infty{A^r\frac{1}{z^{r+1}}\bar{\mathbf{f}}}, \]

whose inverse z-transform is given by

\[ \sum_{r=0}^\infty A^r\mathbf{f}_{k-r-1}u(k-r-1) = \sum_{r=0}^{k-1}A^r\mathbf{f}_{k-r-1}. \]

Example 3.6.1 Consider the difference equation

\[ x_{k+1} = \frac{1}{3}x_k+1 \quad :\quad x_0=1. \]
\[\begin{split} x_k &= \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^k\cdot 1 + \sum_{r=0}^{k-1}\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^r\cdot 1,\\ &=\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^k+\frac{1-\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^k}{1-\frac{1}{3}},\\ &= \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^k+\frac{3}{2}\left[1-\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^k\right]=\frac{3}{2}-\frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^k. \end{split}\]

Example 3.6.2 Consider

\[\begin{split} A = \begin{pmatrix}2&1&1\\-1&0&-1\\3&5&4\end{pmatrix} = HDH^{-1}, \end{split}\]

where

\[\begin{split} H=\begin{pmatrix}1&-1&1\\0&1&-1\\-1&-1&2\end{pmatrix}, H^{-1}=\begin{pmatrix}1&1&0\\1&3&1\\1&2&1\end{pmatrix} ~\&~ D=\begin{pmatrix}1&0&0\\0&2&0\\0&0&3\end{pmatrix}. \end{split}\]

Here

\[\begin{split} A^r = HD^rH^{-1} = \begin{pmatrix}1&-1&1\\0&1&-1\\-1&-1&2\end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix}1&0&0\\0&2^r&0\\0&0&3^r\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}1&1&0\\1&3&1\\1&2&1\end{pmatrix}. \end{split}\]

In this case it is probably worthwhile to determine \(A^r\) in the light of the example to be considered.

Now

\[\begin{split} D^rH^{-1} = \begin{pmatrix}1&0&0\\0&2^r&0\\0&0&3^r\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}1&1&0\\1&3&1\\1&2&1\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}1&1&0\\2^r&3\cdot2^r&2^r\\3^r&2\cdot3^r&3^r\end{pmatrix}, \end{split}\]

and

\[\begin{split} A^r &= HD^rH^{-1}=\begin{pmatrix}1&-1&1\\0&1&-1\\-1&-1&2\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}1&1&0\\2^r&3\cdot2^r&2^r\\3^r&2\cdot3^r&3^r\end{pmatrix},\\ &=\begin{pmatrix}1-2^r+3^r&1-3\cdot2^r+2\cdot3^r&-2^r+3^r\\2^r-3^r&3\cdot2^r-2\cdot3^r&2^r-3^r\\-1-2^r+2\cdot3^r&-1-3\cdot2^r+4\cdot3^r&-2^r+2\cdot3^r\end{pmatrix}. \end{split}\]

Suppose now that in the system (16), \(\displaystyle\mathbf{f}_k=[1,0,0]^T\) and \(\displaystyle\mathbf{x}_0=[0,-1,2]^T\).

Then, using

\[ \mathbf{x}_k = A^k\mathbf{x}_0+\sum_{r=0}^{k-1}A^r\mathbf{f}_{k-1-r}, \]

we have

\[\begin{split} A^k\mathbf{x}_0 = \begin{pmatrix}1-2^k+3^k&1-3\cdot2^k+2\cdot3^k&-2^k+3^k\\2^k-3^k&3\cdot2^k-2\cdot3^k&2^k-3^k\\-1-2^k+2\cdot3^k&-1-3\cdot2^k+4\cdot3^k&-2^k+2\cdot3^k\end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix}0\\-1\\2\end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix}-1+2^k\\-2^k\\1+2^k\end{pmatrix}, \end{split}\]

and

\[\begin{split} \sum_{r=0}^{k-1}A^r\mathbf{f}_{k-1-r} &= \sum_{r=0}^{n-1} \begin{pmatrix}1-2^r+3^r&1-3\cdot2^r+2\cdot3^r&-2^r+3^r\\2^r-3^r&3\cdot2^r-2\cdot3^r&2^r-3^r\\-1-2^r+2\cdot3^r&-1-3\cdot2^r+4\cdot3^r&-2^r+2\cdot3^r\end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix}1\\0\\0\end{pmatrix},\\ &=\sum_{r=0}^{k-1}\begin{pmatrix}1-2^r+3^r\\2^r-3^r\\-1-2^r+2\cdot3^r\end{pmatrix}. \end{split}\]

Now,

\[ \sum_{r=0}^{k-1}1=k,\sum_{r=0}^{k-1}2^r=2^k-1 \text{ and } \sum_{r=0}^{k-1}3^r=\frac{3^k-1}{2}. \]

Therefore

\[\begin{split} \mathbf{x}_k &= \begin{pmatrix}-1+2^k\\-2^k\\1+2^k\end{pmatrix} + \begin{pmatrix} k-(2^k-1)+\frac{3^k-1}{2}\\2^k-1-\frac{3^k-1}{2}\\-k-(2^k-1)+(3^k-1)\end{pmatrix},\\ &=\begin{pmatrix} -\frac{1}{2}+k+\frac{3^k}{2},&-\frac{1}{2}-\frac{3^k}{2},&1-k+3^k\end{pmatrix}^T. \end{split}\]
